Integrate a premium, non-intrusive advertising system into the social media platform “Zorish – Apna Social Space”.

The ads must NOT look spammy, flashy, or cheap.
Maintain the Cosmic Premium Dark theme.

-------------------------------------
AD DESIGN PRINCIPLES
-------------------------------------

• Ads must blend naturally into the feed.
• No banner ads.
• No pop-up interstitial ads.
• No flashing or bright external colors.
• Maintain same card design as normal posts.
• Ad density: 1 ad per 8 posts maximum.
• Clearly label ads with a small “Sponsored” tag.

-------------------------------------
NATIVE FEED AD IMPLEMENTATION
-------------------------------------

Insert sponsored posts inside the main feed.

Placement logic:
After every 8 organic posts, insert 1 sponsored post.

Sponsored Post Design:

Card Background:
#121A3A

Border:
rgba(255,255,255,0.08)

Border Radius:
16px

Header:
Profile image (brand logo)
Brand name
Small muted “Sponsored” label in gold (#F5B041)

Content:
Image or short text content
Optional CTA button

CTA Button:
Background: #F5B041
Text: #0B1026
Rounded (20px)

No external bright ad styling allowed.

-------------------------------------
MOBILE AD RULES
-------------------------------------

• Do not place ads at the very top of feed.
• First ad appears only after user scrolls.
• Maintain smooth scroll performance.
• Avoid layout shifts.

-------------------------------------
EXPLORE PAGE ADS
-------------------------------------

Allow sponsored content on Explore page.

Placement:
1 sponsored card per 10 content cards.

Same design style as feed ads.

-------------------------------------
STORY ADS (OPTIONAL)
-------------------------------------

Insert sponsored story between organic stories.

Display small “Ad” badge in top corner.
No forced full-screen video ads.

-------------------------------------
BACKEND LOGIC
-------------------------------------

Create ad flag system:
Each feed item should have:
isSponsored: boolean

If true:
Render sponsored layout.

Ensure ads are dynamically inserted based on frequency rules.

-------------------------------------
PREMIUM SUBSCRIPTION LOGIC
-------------------------------------

If user has premium subscription:
Do not render ads.

-------------------------------------
IMPORTANT
-------------------------------------

Ads must feel native, elegant, and consistent with Zorish brand.

The UI must still feel like a premium startup product.
Not like a low-budget ad-heavy app.
